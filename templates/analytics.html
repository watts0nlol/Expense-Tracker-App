Unusual Spending Detection

This automatically compares your spending per category this month against your historical average. If any category is 50% or more above your usual average, it gets flagged here.

Requirements: You need at least 2 months of recorded expenses for a category before it can be compared. The more months of data you have, the more accurate the detection becomes.

No setup needed - just record your expenses regularly and check back here each month. Any notifications generated are also saved under the Notifications section below.


Spending Goals

Set a spending limit for a category in a given month. The tracker will show how much you have spent versus your limit so you can stay on track.

How to set one up: Choose a month, pick a category (e.g. Food & Dining), enter a dollar limit, and click Set Goal. You can set one goal per category per month. To update a limit just set it again - it will overwrite the existing one.
